
**Start of AI - Mainly text conversation some degree of speech:**
    
**1960s: Introduction of Ai_Voice_Assistance :**
    It started with the turning machine and shoebox.
**1961 – IBM Shoebox :**
    IBM shoebox was an expiermental device designed to help machines understand human speech. Shoebox could understand 16 spoken words which was a great improvment.
**1966 – ELIZA :**
    Eliza a machine that used methods of recognizing language patterns to mimic human-like conversations
**1988 – Jabberwacky :**
    AI assistants witnessed proggresive growth as Jabberwacky is a "First" Chatbot and its stated to aim to simulate natural human chat in a humourous manner
**1995 – ALICE :**
    ALICE is a language proccesing chatbot that portrays a young woman who talks about her hobbies and answer your question.
**1999 – Clippy (Microsoft Office Assistant) :**
    Everyone who has worked on computers in the late 90s can visually remember the little paperclip, which practically served no purpose but was a fascinating edition. Clippy suggests random changes in your text input
**2001 – SmarterChild :**
    The early 2000s brought the era of commercial chatbots. Large-scale businesses began incorporating AI assistants for handling online customer support

**AI talking era - already fully capable of speech:**

**2011 – Apple Siri :**
     was the first voice assistance that talked back to the user and gave them the information she uses voice queries, gesture based control and a natural-language user interface to answer questions and make recommendations. Siri also adapts to the individual user their language usage, searches and preferences to return the best possible result for each user
**2012 – Google Now :**
    Google’s first-ever virtual assistant, Google Now, was the company’s attempt to provide personalized AI assistance. It came with voice search functionality and was integrated with Google Search to offer relevant updates based on user activities
**2015 – Microsoft Cortana :**
    Microsoft Cortana is a cloud-based personal assistant that operates outside the realm of standard voice-enabled AI. Cortana doesn't just understand voice commands and carry out tasks but is integrated for use across Microsoft's 365 suite of products
**2018 – Google Duplex AI :**
    Google Duplex was launched in 2018, as the AI technology to assist users with real-world tasks like appointment scheduling, handling elaborate conversations, etc.
___________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________
**2020s - AI Assistants & Generative AI:**
    The digital world keeps on expanding its capabilities, and that’s what has led to the current generative AI era
**2022 – ChatGPT :**
    ChatGPT is an AI model created by OpenAI that is capable of conversation in text or in voice. The smart tool can answer questions, converse with people, generate content, and generate images. It learns based on your conversations and tries to satisfy you with the best answer possible. Its currently the most used AI model
**2023 – Microsoft 365 Copilot :**
    Microsoft 365 Copilot a perfect assistant for comprehensive management across its entire ecosystem. It claims to transform productivity in many ways across multiple Microsoft applications. It will assist users in creative tasks by optimizing PowerPoint presentations and improving analysis and data visualization on Excel sheets and much more

**Keyfigures and Milestones:**
1950 - Alan Turing : The first concept of intelligent machines
1961 - William C. Dersch : Developer of IBM shoebox and the first voice recognition device
1966 - Joseph Weizenbaum : Creator of the first chatbot ELIZA
1988 - Rollo Carpenter : Creator of Jabberwacky first "human" speech machine
1995 - Richard Wallace : Developer of Alice, language-processing chatbot and a foundational chatbot in AI conversations
2011 - Apple : Siri the first voice assistant that talked back to the user !!!
2022 - OpenAI : The well known ChatGPT that revolutionized the use of AI
